About Me


I’m a junior developer that is constantly pursuing to educate myself in the Tech industry. I spent time at Ivy Tech Community College and Eleven Fifty Academy working with both front and back end development. I believe life is a learning experience that requires us to seek out solutions to life’s difficult problems. Programming and app development allows us to connect with the world to help educate ourselves to reach these solutions. I want to be apart of that solution. I’m eager and willing to learn more for my career. Working for the public for 8 years in hospitality, I know how to communicate, and prioritize time with coworkers and customers. Being able to provide people a positive experience through interactions within work and in app development are crucial to me. Creating applications that can help change the world is what I want my career in software development to be.
